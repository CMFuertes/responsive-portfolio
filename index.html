About Me

Hello! My Name is Cristhal Fuertes. I am currently enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. On days when I'm not wrapping my head around the complexities of this course, I earn my living in healthcare. I am currently a technician in pediatrics at Monmouth Medical Center. My specialties include soothing the fussiest newborns, calming the most frightened teens, as well as pacifying the most flustered parents. Despite my gifts in taking care of fellow humans, I decided it is time for a career change.

I am a frequent traveler. However, after recent events, barring any international travel, I decided it is time to seek work outside of healthcare. I believe it is time to switch gears and move to a career that can easily progress to a remote position. Thus giving my the freedom not awarded to me in an otherwise non-remote position. As the world continues to change at such an accelerated rate, coding seems to be the right fit. I am excited to continue this endeavor in beginning a brand new career.

That being said, stay awhile. Have a look around. Take a look at my portfolio, perhaps I have what you're looking for.
